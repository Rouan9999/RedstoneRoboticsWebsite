About Us

Redstone Robotics is a FIRST Robotics Competition team located at Lee High School and New Century Tech High school. We started in 2000 and are the only team in Huntsville city that is open to students from the community. We currently consist of students and 4 mentors. FRC team 442 has competed in almost every FRC season since 2000. We have won several awards which include the 2000 National Championship Autodesk judges high score award and Peachtree Regional Finalist. For the first time in almost 20 years, our students attended the 2019 FIRST World Championship in Houston, TX.

Our Mission

Our objective is to encourage students to take advantage of opportunities that other schools might not focus on, and providing them with in the hopes of enhancing their STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) skills in addition to other crucial life skills, like working as a team and dedication, which seem to be absolutely essential for an engineering career.

Our Goal

Our goal is to train students to be more affluent in the professional STEM or business fields that they wish to pursue. Subsequently, that’s exactly what students gain in time while on 2471. Students who have incorporated with a division and successfully completed introductory training will begin to explore projects that advance their respective knowledge, push them to seek new and innovative solutions, and initiate their involvement in leadership positions on the team.
