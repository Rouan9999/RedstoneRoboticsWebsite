A FEW WORDS ABOUT US

Redstone Robotics is a FIRST Tech Challenge team based in New Century Tech and Lee High School. We started in 2000 and switched from FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition) to FTC (FIRST Tech Challenge) in 2021. We currently have around 20 members and 2 coaches. Even though we have only competed in FTC for 2 years, we've received multiple awards in outreach and innovation. This year, we are hosting Alabama's FTC State Championship and hope to make it to FIRST World Championship in Houston TX.

WHAT WE DO

Programing

Learn code and create autonomous routines to make the robots come to life in unique and efficient ways.

Machining

Machine and assemble important parts of robots using world class tools such as 3D Printersand CNCs.

Design & Cad

Create designs of the future of the team using professional software like Inventor and Grab CAD.

Public Speaking

Present the team’s successes in front of other students, parents, and even sponsors to further the team’s growth.

Photography and Videography

Work with the rest of the team to capture their work and spirit by using professional equipment

What is FIRST ?

Their mission is to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders, by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership.
